## Example: Power User

**Role:** Senior Developer
**Technical Proficiency:** Expert
**Primary Goal:** Ship features quickly with high quality

### Background

Experienced developer who has been using the product for over a year. Knows the shortcuts and advanced features. Values efficiency and automation.

### Needs & Motivations

- Fast, keyboard-driven workflows
- Customisation options
- Detailed error messages for debugging
- Integration with existing tools

### Pain Points

- Slow or repetitive tasks
- Lack of advanced configuration
- Features that assume beginner knowledge

### Typical Tasks

- Complex multi-step operations
- Batch processing
- Automation and scripting
- Training other team members

### Quote

> "Just let me configure it properly once, and then get out of my way."

## Example: New User

**Role:** Junior Developer
**Technical Proficiency:** Novice
**Primary Goal:** Learn the basics without breaking anything

### Background

Recently joined the team and is learning the product. Needs clear guidance and safe defaults. May be overwhelmed by too many options.

### Needs & Motivations

- Clear, step-by-step guidance
- Safe defaults
- Undo/recovery options
- Visual feedback

### Pain Points

- Cryptic error messages
- Too many options without explanation
- Fear of making irreversible mistakes

### Typical Tasks

- Following tutorials
- Simple CRUD operations
- Learning terminology
- Asking for help

### Quote

> "I just want to know if I'm doing this right."
