**Lesson.** Evidence that makes a loop falsifiable must survive a fresh clone, or the loop is falsifiable only on the machine that happens to hold the file. The forecast log that made the estimator judgeable was written to a gitignored .local/ directory, so on any other checkout every unit reads UNFORECAST and the whole history reads as no-evidence. The derived summaries were committed; the per-unit evidence underneath them was not - and the evidence is what any future decision has to be made from. Ask of every artefact the process depends on: does this exist for a teammate who clones the repo tomorrow?
