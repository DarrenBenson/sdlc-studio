  harness could take the verdict straight from score output (protocol v3 note).

## Lessons

- A prerequisite is not done until its ceremony is done: verify the ledger row, not
  the commit. (Candidate for lessons add --global.)
- When the evidence and the product disagree, split the CR: ship the prose half now,
  engineer the mechanical half properly (CR0232/CR0229 pattern).
- Post-hoc judged measures (the rubric) are worth running when they can be
  cross-validated against a pre-registered oracle - convergence is what makes them
  citable.
